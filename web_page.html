CF TALAVERA DE LA REINA

In this webpage I am going to talk about the Talavera de la Reina football club.
The football club Talavera is a Spanish football team of the citi of Talavera de la Reina.
It is a club born after the disappearance of the historic Talavera Football Club, from which it inherits its fans, its colors, its shield and its quarry.
It is in Second Division B.

THE STADIUM
